How It Works
MONTE
CARLO
We simulated the rest of the season
10,000 times to find out who wins.
1
Simulate Every Game
Each remaining game is simulated using a Poisson distribution — the same model used in sports betting and academic research.
10,000 full seasons simulated
2
Team Strength Model
Each team gets a strength score from 7 factors: points %, recent form, home ice, shot share, save %, xG ratio, and streak.
Regression-derived weights · Season 7 validated
3
Random Outcomes
Better teams score more goals on average, but every game has real randomness — just like actual hockey. OT gives a loser point 50/50.
Home boost: +8% · Avg: 2.8 goals/team/game
4
Count the Results
After 10,000 runs, we tally how often each team finishes 1st (Walter Cup) or top 4 (playoffs).
Accuracy: Spearman ρ = 0.976 on Season 7
