What is the Gold Plan?
Once a team is mathematically eliminated from playoff contention, their remaining regular-season standings points become Gold Plan Points — directly determining their position in the 2026 PWHL Entry Draft. The team with the most Gold Plan Points earns the 1st overall pick in each round.
