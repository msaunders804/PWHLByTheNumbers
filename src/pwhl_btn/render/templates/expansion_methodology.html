METHODOLOGY
How We Ranked Them
THE
SCORECARD
35%
Takeover Tour Attendance
Actual fans who paid to attend PWHL games in that city — revealed preference, not surveys. Normalized across all tour stops.
25%
NHL Infrastructure
NHL team presence, arena capacity utilization, corporate sponsorship depth, and playoff culture. PWHL has partnered with NHL infra since Day 1.
20%
Women's Pro Sports
NWSL and WNBA presence, attendance, and franchise health. Proven spending on women's sports is the strongest market signal available.
10%
Arena Fit
Availability of a right-sized venue (6,000–12,000 seats). NHL arenas score lower — the PWHL works best at human scale.
10%
Geographic Balance
How much the city improves league map coverage. Current PWHL is east-heavy — western and central markets score higher.
Data Sources
Tour attendance — PWHL game database, Seasons 5 + 8
NHL market scores — NHL.com, ESPN attendance reports
Women's sports — NWSL, WNBA official attendance records
Arena data — venue capacity databases, team facility reports
